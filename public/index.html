Our products

Two species. Every application.

We supply rubberwood and acacia solid wood panels in standard and custom dimensions. Both species are plantation-grown, FSC certified on request, and available for consistent container-volume supply.

Rubberwood panels

Light colour with a clean, birch-like appearance. Excellent dimensional stability and consistency. One of the most reliable hardwoods for interior applications and worktops.

Compliance & certifications

Sourced responsibly.
Documented properly.

European importers face increasing regulatory requirements. We understand this — and our supply chain is built to support your compliance needs.

FSC

FSC certified

FSC-certified material is available for both rubberwood and acacia panels. Chain of custody documentation provided. Always confirm availability per order as certification depends on source factory allocation.

EUDR

EUDR compliant

The EU Deforestation Regulation requires full traceability for wood products entering the EU from December 2026. Both rubberwood and acacia are plantation-grown species. We support EUDR due diligence requirements including geolocation documentation from source.

Origin

Plantation-grown

Rubberwood originates from former latex plantations — timber harvested after productive life, not from forest clearance. Acacia is similarly plantation-grown in Vietnam. Both species are well-positioned for responsible sourcing claims.

Production

Modern facilities. Consistent output.

The factories we work with operate modern sanding and finishing lines, maintain strict moisture control processes and apply quality checks at critical production stages.

We evaluate production organisation, material flow, quality systems and management attitude — not marketing presentations. Strong process management is a better indicator of quality than any brochure.
