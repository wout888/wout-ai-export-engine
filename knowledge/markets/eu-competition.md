# EU Market — Competitive Intelligence

**Type:** Factory-direct manufacturer and exporter
**Origin:** Vietnam (founded 2009, Vietnamese + Korean)
**Scale:** 11,000+ workers, 950+ containers/month, 60+ countries
**Products:** Finger joint boards, butcher block countertops, stair components, outdoor furniture, cabinet components
**Species:** Primarily rubberwood

**EU Presence:**
- Office in Amsterdam, Netherlands
- Attended Carrefour International du Bois 2026 (major European wood trade fair)
- Actively targeting European market

**Global Offices:** New York (HQ), Ho Chi Minh City, Netherlands, Singapore, Dubai, Korea, Japan

**Positioning:** "Global Manufacturing Platform" — factory direct, OEM/ODM, private label, scale and certifications

**Website:** Professional, well-structured, B2B focused, strong visual production content

## Key Insight
SSR's Amsterdam office confirms the EU market opportunity is real.
Serious companies don't invest in EU offices without commercial justification.

## Important — Their Actual Model
SSR purchases significant volume from third-party factories, including factories we also source from.
Their "factory direct" and "controlled supply chain" positioning is partly marketing.
In practice, they are also a trading/sourcing operation layered on top of multiple suppliers.
This means:
- Their cost structure includes their own overhead and margin
- We can access the same source quality
- We are more price-competitive than their positioning suggests
- Their scale claims should be taken with caution

## Positioning Against SSR
Do not compete on scale — we will lose.
Compete on:
- Personal relationship and trust
- Multi-factory flexibility
- Proven US market experience
- Buyer-first approach
- Faster response, less bureaucracy
