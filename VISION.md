# Wout's AI Export Engine

## Vision

Build a lean, AI-powered export engine that dramatically reduces the time between discovering a market opportunity and professionally launching it.

The objective is not to build websites.

The objective is to rapidly connect trustworthy factories with the right customers through knowledge, experience, credibility and efficient execution.

## Core Principle

> I use AI to scale my knowledge and experience, not to replace my judgment.

## My Role

My value is not writing code or designing websites.

My value is:

- Finding opportunities
- Visiting factories
- Evaluating products
- Understanding quality
- Understanding pricing
- Building relationships
- Communicating efficiently
- Reducing friction between buyer and factory

AI should amplify these strengths, not replace them.

## Knowledge First

Knowledge is the foundation.

Everything else is generated from that knowledge.

Examples include:

- Websites
- Product brochures
- Product PDFs
- Email drafts
- Marketing material
- Translations
- Sales documentation

The knowledge base is the most valuable asset of the entire system.

## Business Model

The preferred business model is commission-based representation.

The goal is to identify reliable factories, validate them personally, create professional market presence, connect them with the right customers, and earn fair commission for creating value.

Direct trading remains possible when appropriate, but is not the primary objective.

## Personal Standard

I only want to represent products and factories that I personally trust.

My reputation is my most valuable asset.

I want customers to know that I have personally evaluated the opportunity and that I will do everything reasonably possible to make the cooperation successful.

## Design Principles

- Keep overhead extremely low.
- Keep systems simple.
- Reuse everything possible.
- Automate repetitive work.
- Focus on execution.
- Build practical tools, not impressive technology.
- Scale knowledge, not complexity.
- Quality and credibility are more important than volume.

## Long-Term Goal

Create a system that allows many market opportunities to be launched quickly and professionally from anywhere in the world.

The purpose is to multiply the impact of personal experience and trusted relationships through AI.

## Success

Success is measured by sustainable positive cash flow, trusted long-term relationships and repeat business.

Websites, brochures and AI tools are not the goal.

They are instruments that support the real objective: creating value by connecting the right factories with the right customers.

## Guiding Rule

If something does not create real commercial value, it can wait.
